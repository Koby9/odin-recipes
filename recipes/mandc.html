Mac & Cheese

Oh so cheesy, oh so yummy

Macaroni and cheese is one of the greatest dishes ever, on this place we call earth. I have scoured through thousands of mom's pinterest dishes to find the perfect one. And here it is...

Ingredients

First off, we need to gather a few things.

  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Water
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • And a hungry belly

Let's Make it

  1. Get that water boiling. (We reccomend using a BBQ grill, cause why not?)
  2. Water bubbling? Throw them noodles in there.
  3. Grab a noodle, throw it at a wall. If sticks, move on to step 4.
  4. Mix butter, milk, and cheese (a lot of cheese).
  5. Let cool, and enjoy.
