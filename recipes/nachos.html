The One, The Only, Ultimate Nachos

So good, you won't want to share.

Nachos are the perfect family meal or appetizer to share at your favorite local restaurant. But not these. Trust me, my wife divorced me because of them. Let's just say I chose them, over her.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla Chips
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Nacho Cheese Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Sliced Olives
  • Tomato

Let's make it!

  1. Lay your chips on your plate with a layer of shredded cheddar in the middle and top.
  2. Pour a hefty amount of nacho cheese sauce on your tortilla chips.
  3. Microwave it.
  4. Top as desired.
  5. Enjoy while you try to fend off the onslaught of nacho thieves.
