Good Ol' Spaghetti

"Wow, it looks so delicious" - your date

There is not much to say about spaghetti. I mean it's spaghetti. So hope you enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Water
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Meatballs
  • Parmesean Cheese

Let's Make It!

  1. Boil some water.
  2. Put noodles in boiling water.
  3. Strain noodles in strainer.
  4. Add sauce and meatballs to the noodles.
  5. Don't forget to shred some parmesean cheese.
  6. Let cool and, ENJOY!
